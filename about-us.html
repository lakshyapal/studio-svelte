Studio Svelte, is a professional, creative & Dynamic- Architectural & Interior Design firm in practice for almost three decades now with multifaceted approach & innovative design ideas.

We are expert at designing a variety of interiors that are reflection of our client's vision, taste and lifestyle. Many of our projects look modern while others have distinctly old European feel to them. We work in all styles i.e classical, Contemporary, Modern, Eclectic and Earthy etc.

Handling Architectural & Interior design projects on pan India basis in field of individual housing (Villas, Residences, Farm houses, Apartments, Penthouses etc). Group Housing High rise housing, Townships, Hotels, Hospitals, Offices as well a Corporate parks, Restaurent, Cafe, Bars and Corporate parks.

Our Mission
To create lively,diverse, and safe cities and neighborhoods
To improve people's lives - rather than focusing on design aesthetics alone
To consider,develop and accentuate amenities
To achieve the above while considering and accounting for climatic and environmental concers
Ensuring sustainability in all aspects: social, physical, mental, environmental & economical
History
1987
Launched new concept based product into white goods industy in india under Vacline Appliances (rechristened studio svelte)
A mix marketing strategy in the first year itself combining the direct sales force & aptly using distributed channels launched = Uninational brand of vacuum cleaners,giving Euroclean & Hoover a run for its money in the territories whereever it was launched.
Next Launch by vacline appliances the first electric chimney in india of alex mac, which was subsequently copied by deeshe & ultrafresh brand of chimneys
3rd launch by vacline appliances. Prestige food warmers line establishing a record 45 dealer network in 30 days in delhi & demand from the very 2nd month climbing to 200 pcs/month
Vacline appliances acted as a marketing organization for various small scale manufacturers (unable to attain to demand pressure)
Quest of launching new products & concepts continued by Vacline Appliances by acquiring the rights for direct marketing in Northern India for Essentia Brand of Microwave from Bharat Forge Ltd.
Availability of team of carpentry for alteration & installation of Electric Chimney & failure of small scale manufacturers to provide the desired numbers & quality of Product marketed, the management took a decision to launch MODULAR KITCHEN CABINETS in Indian market- another first in concept selling of product & cabinetry as product
1992
Having set up a small workshop for the manufacturing facility for Modular Kitchen Cabinets, using ecofriendly materials; the success & appreciation was overwhelming.
1993
In the year 1993 ,a conscious decision was taken by the management to shut down the White Goods Division & focus on Modular Kitchen Cabinets.
1995
Complete Project Management Services introduced including Architectural Services offered
1996-1997
Further diversification to Custom Built Furniture, Cabinetry & Complete Home & Office interiors due to pressing demands of our clients. The architectural services rendered also earned a distinctive mark in the field.
2005
Introduced one stop solution which means from design to completion.
2007
A real estate division providing services of plots , cottages, studio apartments in the hills of kumaon
